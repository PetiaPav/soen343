What is Booker LMS?

Booker LMS, a Library Management System, provides on-line access to a Library catalog that any logged-in user may explore. The catalog is filled with wonders such as printed and media items, namely, books, magazines, music and movies. Pick and choose from the large selection of items of all genres and loan it to enjoy it. All items are loanable if available, at the exception of magazines.
